AEROGRID SOLUTIONS PRIVATE LIMITED - CORPORATE CAPABILITY STATEMENT
================================================================================

1. COMPANY OVERVIEW
AeroGrid Solutions Private Limited is a premier engineering, procurement, and construction (EPC) company and class-A equipment manufacturer specializing in high-voltage electrical infrastructure and green energy integration. Established in 2015, we design, manufacture, and install state-of-the-art power transformers, distribution panels, and solar grid-tie substations.

2. CORE COMPETENCIES
- High-Voltage Transformers: Manufacturing and testing of power transformers up to 220kV, 100MVA.
- Grid Integration: Complete design and execution of ISTS/InSTS substation packages.
- Technical Compliance: All products comply with IEC 60076, IS 2026, and Bureau of Energy Efficiency (BEE) 5-star ratings.
- Annual Manufacturing Capacity: Over 1,500 MVA cumulative capacity of power and distribution transformers.

3. PAST PERFORMANCE & WINNING BIDS (REFERENCE PROJECTS)
- Project Alpha (2023): Supply, installation, and commissioning of 12 Units of 50MVA, 132/33kV Power Transformers for NTPC Solar Power Evacuation Project in Rajasthan. Value: INR 28.5 Crores. (Status: Completed 3 months ahead of schedule).
- Project Beta (2024): Rate Contract for supply of 33/11kV Distribution Transformers for Karnataka Renewable Energy Development Limited (KREDL). Total Units: 85. Value: INR 14.2 Crores. (Status: Completed successfully with 100% compliance).

4. KEY RESUMES & LEADERSHIP
- Dr. Arshia Mehta (Director of Engineering): Over 20 years of experience in power systems design. Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering from IIT Bombay. Leads transformer core and winding optimization.
- Rajesh Varma (Chief Project Manager): 15+ years managing utility-scale substation installation and grid-interconnection commissioning. Certified Project Management Professional (PMP).

5. QUALITY MANAGEMENT & CERTIFICATIONS
- ISO 9001:2015 (Quality Management System)
- ISO 14001:2015 (Environmental Management System)
- NABL Accredited In-House Transformer Testing Laboratory
